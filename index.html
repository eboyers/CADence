What We Do

Format Translation

Fast, loss‑less conversion between CATIA, SOLIDWORKS, NX, Creo, STEP, IGES and more, all without opening the native CAD app.

Automated Validation

AI‑driven geometry and metadata checks guarantee design intent survives translation by detecting unit mismatches, missing features, and assembly drift.

Geometric Healing

Automatic stitching and gap‑filling repairs imperfect B‑Rep models before they hit the shop floor or simulation pipeline.

About CADence

Founded in 2025 by two Stanford students, CADence exists to eliminate the friction of multi‑CAD collaboration. Our platform combines state‑of‑the‑art geometry kernels with large language models to iteratively refine each translation until it is truly indistinguishable from the original. Whether you’re a Tier‑1 supplier, a contract manufacturer, or a robotics startup, CADence keeps your digital thread intact across every tool in your stack.
