About CADence

Founded in 2025 by two Stanford students, CADence exists to eliminate the friction of multi‑CAD collaboration. Our platform combines state‑of‑the‑art geometry kernels with large language models to iteratively refine each translation until it is truly indistinguishable from the original. Whether you’re a Tier‑1 supplier, a contract manufacturer, or a robotics startup, CADence keeps your digital thread intact across every tool in your stack.
